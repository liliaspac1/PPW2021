Moda

Fashion Moda was an art space located in the South Bronx, New York founded by Stefan Eins in 1978.
As a museum of science, art, invention, technology, and fantasy, it was an alternative art space that combined aspects of a community arts center and a worldwide progressive arts organization until its closing in 1993.
